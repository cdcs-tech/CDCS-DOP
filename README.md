## Version

**1.0.0-alpha**

## Overview

The CDCS Digital Operations Platform (CDCS-DOP) is an enterprise digital management system developed for Career Development & Consultancy Services (CDCS).

The platform is designed to integrate organizational operations into one centralized system, including:

- Records Management
- Client Management
- Project Management
- Human Resources
- Finance
- Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL)
- Training Management
- Recruitment
- Procurement
- Reporting & Analytics
- System Administration
